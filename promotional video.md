## 1. Basic Information

- App Name: `MyCRM`
- Logo (PNG/SVG): `assets/logo.png`, `assets/logo1.png`, `assets/applogo.png`
- Tagline: No fixed tagline is defined in the app code. Suggested promo tagline: `Manage leads, projects, tasks, and renewals in one place.`

- Secondary Color: `#18C6D3`

## Production Notes

- Keep the same phone mockup throughout the full sequence
- Keep brand colors consistent: deep navy `#122B52`, cyan `#18C6D3`, white, and light blue surfaces
- Show realistic in-app motion only, not generic floating UI panels
- Use actual CRM actions: login, browse leads, open project, check tasks, review renewals, receive notifications
- Keep transitions seamless so the clips can be stitched together into one polished ad
- Avoid showing features not visible in the app unless you intentionally want a future-ready concept shot
